Intellectual Property

The project will have an Open Source Hardware and Software licensed under MIT license.

I choose this type of license because it permits people to share knowledge and data in easy and clear way and let people to continue devoloping this installation to many propouses.

The key points of the MIT license are:

1.The project is delivered "as is" with no warranty or responsibility whatsoever;
2.Do whatever you want with the code;
3.Remember, give attribution.

Then I choose a Creative Commons license for the Design.

Income and dissemination

I plan to disseminate and present the project at the following upcoming events:

-FAB11 Conference
-wearable Camp
-Rome Maker Faire
-Fashion electronics Conference-Events

I was thinking a dissemination plan for my project as covered the following points:

identification of a target audience for the project

Esole could be useful to a large target of audience: older people, teenagers, ladies loving fashion electronics, geek girls, etc.. But I think that the combo insole plus shoe plus electronic module looks like japanese style, so asiatican people could be favorite users.

brand creation

The logo concept is already defined, I believe to have followed some golden rules in its creation:

-short easy to remember name (E-sole)
-friendly graphics, two colors, easy to identify
-long techy acronomous for appealing users (Electronic Sole)

define an "exploitation" plan, as business model definition, licensing, and extensive evaluation of fashion market opportunities

So first of all I would try to create the possibility for myself to work ahead with the project. To do that I’d search support through crowdfunding, I’d search someone interested in my idea and who could invests on it.I think that for this porpouse equity crowdfunding sistem like Goteo is the good choise.
